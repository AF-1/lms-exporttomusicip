	EN	Adjust odd ratings to LMS rating scale

PLUGIN_EXPORTTOMUSICIP_SETTINGS_ADJUSTRATINGS_DESC
	EN	LMS uses a rating scale with values ranging from zero (no stars) to 100 (5 stars) and 10 for every additional half-star (Examples: 20 = 1 star, 50 = 2.5 stars).<br><br>If your LMS database includes odd rating values (like 4 or 67), i.e. not evenly divisible by 10, you can use this setting to <b>export all odd rating values as rounded values that comply with the LMS standard rating scale</b> (evenly divisible by 10).<br>Example: 4 => 0 and 67 => 70.<br><br>This will only adjust only rating values exported to MusicIP, not those stored in the LMS database.
